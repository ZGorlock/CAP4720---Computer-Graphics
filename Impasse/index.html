IMPASSE

Created By: Zachary Gill

Impasse is a game played on a checkers set that was invented by Mark Steere.
For detailed instructions see: Detailed Rules

There are three basic moves:

  • Slides: Moving one of your single pieces away from you
  • Double Slides: Moving one of your double pieces towards you
  • Transposes: Moving a piece from a double to a single that is closer to you and adjacent to the double


If you move a single to the far side of the board, you must crown it with one of your other singles, turning it into a double.
If you a move a double to the near side of the board, you bear-off the crown and remove the piece from play.
If you have no basic moves available you are at an Impasse, and you may remove any single piece from play or turn any double into a single.


The first person to remove all their pieces from play wins.
Good luck!
