DISGRUNTLED CANNONBALLS

Created By: Zachary Gill

These cannonballs are so disgruntled, you don't want to hear about it here
Make them less disgruntled by knocking down those targets, also you'll get points for doing that:

1 Point : Hitting a target
5 Points : An orb falling to the floor

Control the cannon:
WASD to turn and raise the cannon
Space to fire
L to reload
Q to change ammo
C to change camera perspectives

There are 4 levels, beat all the levels to win!
You must knock all the orbs off the field to move to the next level!
Get the most points with the least amount of cannonballs and you'll be the best!

READY, SET, GO!
